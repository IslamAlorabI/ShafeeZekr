# Privacy Policy for ShafeeZekr

**Last Updated: January 28, 2026**

## Introduction

ShafeeZekr ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.** ShafeeZekr is designed with privacy as a core principle.

### What We Don't Collect
- No personal information (name, email, phone number)
- No location data
- No usage analytics or tracking
- No advertising identifiers
- No device identifiers
- No crash reports sent externally

### Data Stored Locally on Your Device
The app stores the following data **only on your device** using Android's DataStore:
- Your reminder preferences (interval timing)
- Theme and color scheme preferences
- Language preferences
- Sound selection and volume settings
- Quiet hours configurations

This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Is not accessible to us or any third party
- Is deleted when you uninstall the app

## Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| `POST_NOTIFICATIONS` | Display reminder notifications |
| `SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM` / `USE_EXACT_ALARM` | Schedule precise reminder timings |
| `RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED` | Restore reminders after device restart |
| `REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS` | Ensure reliable reminder delivery |
| `INTERNET` | Not used for data transmission; included for potential future features |

## Third-Party Services

ShafeeZekr does **not** use any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics tools. The app is completely self-contained.

## Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is suitable for all ages and contains only Islamic religious reminder content.
